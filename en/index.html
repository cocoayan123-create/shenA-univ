DEPARTMENTS

Departments

Nine departments of one lineage. Masculine Breath Studies opens first; the rest follow in turn.

Masculine Breath Studies Open

Our founding faculty, where the syllabus is a single exhale and every undergraduate learns to read the mood folded into one man’s breathing.

ASMR & Auditory Devotion

A research-grade study of triggers, mouth sounds and the close-miked whisper — lulling the ear to the very edge of sleep and not one inch past propriety.

Acoustic Engineering

Recording, editing and scoring held to laboratory rigor, because a sound that makes you blush should first be mastered to broadcast standard.

Live Companionship

Advanced fieldwork in real-time intimacy and whispered correspondence — a tutorial in presence, timing and the fine art of knowing where to stop.

Synthetic Voice & AI

Where we teach the machine to breathe, to hesitate, to lean in close — and grade it, sternly, on how convincingly it learns restraint.

Script & Dramaturgy

The school of the suggestive sentence, where students draft scenarios that say everything by implication and never spell it out.

Visual Design

Covers, type and palette composed with a curator’s eye, dressing the unspeakable in a respectability tasteful enough to frame.

Operations & Engagement

The pragmatic faculty of release schedules, audience cultivation and growth — proving that even a blush can be run on a curriculum.

Theory & Appreciation

The contemplative wing, where listening becomes scholarship and we defend, with footnotes, why a single sigh deserves a seminar.

FACULTY RECRUITMENT

Now Hiring · Department Chairs & Counsellors

Of our nine departments, only Breath & Voice has taken office. The other eight sit vacant, awaiting their heads — we invite creators in every field to chair them; counsellors are recruited year-round. We judge the work, not the pedigree.

Positions
Department Chair (one per the eight open departments) · Counsellors (several) · Guest Lecturers (by topic)
Duties
Lead your department’s curriculum and creative output; mentor students in their work; speak for the department in public.
Requirements
Public work in your field you can stand behind; respect for women’s-oriented work and a sense of where the line sits. No degree required — we look at the craft.
We Offer
Credit and a share of the returns; full creative freedom; and a name that takes this work seriously.
